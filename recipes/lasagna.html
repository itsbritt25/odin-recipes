Delicious Homeade Lasagna

Easy to make lasagna

This recipe is delicious and easy to make, even for beginners.

Let's make a lasagna the whole family will enjoy, even the little ones!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1(28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1(8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1(16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1(16 ounce) package shredded mozerella cheese

Steps:

  1. Combine pork and ground beef in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until browned.
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles, stirring occasionally, under tender. Preheat oven to 375 degree F, while noodles are cooking. Drain and set aside.
  5. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, 1 tablespoon parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined.
  6. Assemble lasagna: Spread a spoon or two of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13- inch baking dish just to coat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover
  7. Layer with 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the mozerella cheese. Repeat layers once more using the remaining noodles, cheese mixture, sauce, and mozerella. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5-10 more minutes.
  9. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
